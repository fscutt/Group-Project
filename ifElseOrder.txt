Tones(interval)
  - root isnt affected by anything
  - third can either be major(3) or minor(4)
  - fifth can be perfect(7),augmented,(8) or diminished(6)
  - seventh can be major(11), minor(10), or diminished(9)
  - 9th can be major(2), minor(1)
  - add 9 adds the 9th and removes the 7th
  - 11th can be perfect(5)
  - add 11 

variables
  - assume major if nothing
  - minor present flattens third
  - minor and major present flattens third but additions are major
  - augmenting raises fifth
